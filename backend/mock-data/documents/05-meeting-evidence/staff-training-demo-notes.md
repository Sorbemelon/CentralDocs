# Staff Training Demo Notes

**Project:** Orchid Retail Digital Transformation  
**Mock media file:** `staff-training-demo.mp4`  
**Purpose:** Demonstrates CentralDocs video handling with a short visual training clip related to the same mock document corpus.

## Video Summary

This short training demo explains how Orchid Retail staff should use CentralDocs during the digital transformation rollout.

The demo covers five steps:

1. Open the demo workspace and review the connected folders.
2. Attach a folder or selected documents to a chat session.
3. Ask a grounded question about vendor onboarding, invoice tracking, or support issues.
4. Expand the references panel to confirm which files informed the answer.
5. Generate a new briefing document from the saved chat and download it.

## Key Message

CentralDocs is designed to turn scattered business documents into searchable knowledge, then turn useful AI conversations back into reusable documents.
