# Rollout Risk Discussion Transcript

**Project:** Orchid Retail Digital Transformation  
**Mock media file:** `rollout-risk-discussion.mp3`  
**Purpose:** Demonstrates CentralDocs audio handling with a short internal meeting note related to the same mock document corpus.

## Transcript

Maya from Operations opened the discussion by saying that Orchid Retail's biggest rollout risk is not the AI search itself, but inconsistent document habits across teams. Finance stores vendor forms in spreadsheets, Support keeps repeated answers in ticket notes, and store managers still send policy updates through chat.

Arun from IT said CentralDocs should start with three controlled folders: Strategy and Rollout, Finance and Vendors, and Customer and Support Signals. He recommended limiting public uploads during the demo, but keeping the mock files broad enough to show PDF, spreadsheet, slides, image, audio, and video support.

Lina from Finance added that invoice tracking and vendor onboarding should be part of the first demo because those files are often requested together. The group agreed that every AI answer should show references used, so staff can confirm whether the answer came from a policy, checklist, feedback export, or meeting evidence.

The next action is to prepare a short briefing for department leads explaining the rollout risks, selected folders, and reference-backed AI chat behavior.
