# Example Generated Chat Summary

## Instruction

Summarize the Orchid Retail digital transformation discussion into a concise internal briefing with references.

## Background

Orchid Retail is piloting CentralDocs to centralize policies, vendor workflows, invoice tracking, customer support knowledge, and meeting evidence into one AI-assisted document workspace.

## Key findings

- Document search is currently fragmented across folders, emails, spreadsheets, and meeting files.
- Vendor onboarding and invoice tracking are common pain points.
- Customer support teams need faster access to approved answers.
- Managers want AI answers that cite the exact documents used.

## Risks

- Staff may over-attach documents and receive unfocused answers.
- Audio and video files should be indexed once and reused in the demo.
- Generated documents must retain references to remain trustworthy.

## Next steps

1. Train staff to attach folders before asking broad questions.
2. Show the References Used panel in every demo.
3. Generate a short briefing after each pilot chat.
4. Keep generated documents downloadable and searchable.
