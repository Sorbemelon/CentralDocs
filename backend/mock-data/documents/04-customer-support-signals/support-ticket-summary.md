# Support Ticket Summary

## Overview

This fictional summary consolidates recent Orchid Retail support tickets related to document access, invoice status, and repeated internal questions.

## Common issue themes

1. Staff cannot find the latest vendor onboarding checklist.
2. Store managers ask where invoice status should be tracked.
3. Remote approvals are delayed because approvers cannot locate the right SOP.
4. Support agents repeat answers that already exist in the playbook.
5. Customer-facing staff need short summaries instead of long policy files.

## Recommended actions

- Keep vendor and invoice files in the Finance & Vendors folder.
- Attach folders to CentralDocs chat when asking cross-document questions.
- Use references-used panels to verify where answers came from.
- Generate short briefings after long chat sessions.
- Keep generated documents searchable for future reuse.
