# CentralDocs Transformation Brief

## Company

Orchid Retail is a fictional regional retail company with 24 stores and a growing online support channel.

## Problem

Orchid Retail's internal documents are scattered across email threads, local drives, shared folders, spreadsheets, meeting recordings, and slide decks. Staff frequently ask the same questions about vendor onboarding, invoice tracking, document naming, remote approvals, and customer support workflows.

## Transformation goal

The company wants a central document workspace where teams can upload files, organize folders, search by meaning, chat with selected documents, cite sources, and turn useful conversations into new reusable documents.

## Success criteria

- Reduce repeated support questions about document procedures.
- Make invoice and vendor workflows easier to find.
- Give staff one place to locate policies, SOPs, and meeting evidence.
- Preserve references used by AI answers.
- Let managers generate short briefing documents from chat sessions.

## Pilot scope

The first pilot includes Document Operations, Finance & Vendors, Customer Support, and the Digital Workspace rollout team.

## Known risks

- Staff may attach too many documents and get unfocused answers.
- Audio/video files should be indexed once and reused to avoid high AI usage.
- The finance team needs clear source references before trusting generated summaries.
- Old folder habits may continue unless the demo guide is visible.
